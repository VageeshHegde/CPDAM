Director's Message


Our Objectives

Objectives:

  1. To be the region's premier hub for product design and development, technology commercialization, and manufacturing.
  2. To establish a common equipment and testing laboratory of equipment available to the region's industry, community, FSU employees, and students.
  3. To provide services to the region's small, medium, and large businesses.
  4. To assist startup companies in the region with product development.
  5. To provide training and workshops on the use of equipment and advanced software to the industry, community, FSU employees, and students.
  6. To engage students in real-world cutting-edge knowledge in system and product design, and advanced manufacturing.
  7. To make use of the center's technical assets and experience from Frostburg State University's faculty, staff, and students.

About Us


Our vision is to develop an advanced design, manufacturing, and synchrony emerging technology center that will support industrial innovation through system and product design, prototyping and advanced manufacturing. CPDAM is envisioned to have cutting-edge machining and design capabilities to fulfill its objectives.

The CPDAM seeks to be a regional leader in industrial innovation, technological advancement, and service by providing integrated design, manufacturing, and synchrony emerging technologies in the development of products and systems. CPDAM will bridge the gap between FSU, regional industry, and the community through the deployment of commercialization solutions.
